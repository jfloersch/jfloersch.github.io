Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

OpenBookshelf is built on a simple principle: your reading life is your own business. The app operates entirely on your device and collects no data whatsoever.

Data collection

OpenBookshelf does not collect any data. We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, reading history, search queries, or any other personal or anonymous information.

Third-party services

OpenBookshelf uses the OpenLibrary API to search for book metadata. When you search for a book, a request is sent to OpenLibrary's servers. This is governed by OpenLibrary's own policies. No personal information is included in these requests.

Analytics & tracking

OpenBookshelf uses no analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.

Advertising

There are no ads in OpenBookshelf. There are no ad networks, no ad SDKs, and no data shared for advertising purposes.

Your data stays on your device

All bookshelf data (your shelves, reading status, and notes) is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to any server operated by FE Studio or any third party.
