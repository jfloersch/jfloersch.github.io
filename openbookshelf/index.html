How it works

Track your reading in three taps.

Search for a book, add it to your shelf, mark it as read. That's it. No onboarding wizard, no account creation, no tutorial. Just your books.

Build your library

Search millions of books powered by Open Library — the world's largest open book database. Add books instantly by title, author, or ISBN. No proprietary database, no lock-in.

Import from Goodreads

Bring your entire reading history with you. Export your Goodreads data and import it into OpenBookshelf in seconds. Keep your shelves, your ratings, your reading dates — without starting over.

Sync across devices

Your library stays up to date on every device automatically with iCloud. Add a book on your iPhone, see it on your iPad. No account required — it just works.

What you won't find here.

No algorithmic recommendations. No social feed. No reading challenges designed to make you feel guilty. No ads. No behavioral tracking. No subscription that charges you monthly to maintain a book list.

OpenBookshelf does one thing well: it helps you keep track of what you've read, what you're reading, and what you want to read next. Nothing more. Nothing less.
