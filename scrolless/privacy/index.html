Scrolless

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026  ·  Fe Studio

Scrolless collects absolutely no data. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device. Nothing is ever transmitted, stored remotely, or shared with anyone — including us.

Who we are

Scrolless is developed and published by Fe Studio, an independent software studio. You can reach us at jared@festudio.net.

What data we collect

None. Scrolless does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or any other information about you or your device.

How Scrolless works

Scrolless is a Safari extension. It runs entirely as a local browser extension on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac. All blocking rules and preferences you configure are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (such as UserDefaults or iCloud sync if you choose to enable it).

At no point does any data — including your browsing activity, blocked URLs, or settings — leave your device or reach Fe Studio's servers. Fe Studio does not operate any servers that receive data from Scrolless.

Third-party services

Scrolless does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. There are no third-party services embedded in the app that could collect your data.

Purchase and payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple's privacy policy governs any data collected during the purchase process. Fe Studio never receives your payment information.

iCloud sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, iOS or macOS may sync your app preferences (such as your blocking rules) across your devices using your personal iCloud account. This sync happens between your devices via Apple's infrastructure and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. Fe Studio cannot access this data.

Children's privacy

Scrolless does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected, Scrolless is compliant with COPPA and similar children's privacy regulations by design.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Scrolless handles data (for example, if we add optional features that involve data collection), we will update this policy and change the effective date above. We will never collect data without updating this policy first.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at jared@festudio.net.
